Spline Interpolation Tool

Instructions

  • Upload a CSV file with waypoints (x, y coordinates per row) or use the manual editor
  • Choose spline type: Natural, Clamped, Periodic, or B-Spline
  • Adjust resolution to control smoothness of the interpolated curve
  • For B-Splines, adjust the degree (2=Quadratic, 3=Cubic, 4=Quartic, 5=Quintic)
  • Click waypoints in the manual editor to modify coordinates
  • Export results as CSV or plot image
